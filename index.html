Biography

I received the B.S. degree in electronics and information engineering from Sichuan University, Chengdu, China, in 2012, where he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaohai He (Sichuan University). From 2015.9 to 2016.10, I was a visiting scholar with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of California at San Diego, CA, USA, under the supervision of Prof. Truong Nguyen.

I got the highest average score (about Top 0.5%, 1/200) in the department of information and communication engineering at Sichuan University during my undergraduate study, and has received various contest awards (e.g., National Undergraduate Electronic Design Contest, 2012) and scholarships (e.g., National Scholarship, 2016).

Research Areas

My research interests include inverse problems in image and video processing, video segmentation, and computer vision.
